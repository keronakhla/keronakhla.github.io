I'm Kerolos. I likes to design and build beautiful iOS apps. my path to becoming an iOS developer was not direct I kind of fell into it. I was a premed student at St. Mary's College of California where I took my first programming class and fell in love with coding and the rest is history...

I'm passionate about the iOS platform and strive to follow its philosophy of building neat solutions on the inside and out. I care about pixel-perfect design as much as object-oriented code. I enjoy transforming static mock-ups into live and responsive forms especially custom interfaces and innovative UI concepts. My focus at the moment is native iOS development using UIKit but I also have some experience building web based applications with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS using Meteor technologies.

I'm always looking to find interesting and challenging projects with new technologies, so this doesn’t mean that I’m limited to the technologies mentioned here.

Skills: Git, Github, Command Line, Objective-C, Swift, Xcode, iOS, iOS Development, Parse, Bohemian Coding - Sketch3, App Prototyping, Scrum, Lean Startup.

You can find out about my current iOS activity on this blog, and see some of the iOS projects I'm working on Github. I'm open to discuss opportunities in iOS development. You can reach out to me on LinkedIn and Twitter. You can also use the Contact form on this website.
